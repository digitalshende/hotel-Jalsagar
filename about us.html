About us

About Us – Hotel Jalsagar: Welcome to Hotel Jalsagar, a beloved dining destination on Satara-Medha-Mahabaleshwar Road in Satara, Maharashtra. With over two decades of service to locals and travelers alike, we have built a reputation for freshly prepared, flavour-rich cuisine with hospitality at its heart.
What We Serve: At Hotel Jalsagar, we celebrate the flavours of Maharashtra and beyond. Our menu features everything from classic dhaba favourites to speciality seafood dishes, prepared with care using fresh ingredients. Guests often praise iconic dishes like fish fry, asmli fry, mutton masala, and surmai tava masala — each dish served with generous portions and authentic taste. .
Atmosphere & Experience: Our restaurant brings together a friendly atmosphere and attentive service to make every visit comfortable and enjoyable. .
Our Commitment: We believe in: Quality and freshness in every dish.
Warm hospitality for all our guests.
Location & Hours: Conveniently located on Satara-Medha-Mahabaleshwar Road, Hotel Jalsagar is open daily from late morning to late evening, ready to welcome you for lunch, dinner, and everything in between.
