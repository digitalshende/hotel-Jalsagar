Welcome to Hotel Jalsagar, Satara

Welcome to Hotel Jalsagar, a popular family restaurant and dhaba-style dining destination located on Satara-Medha-Mahabaleshwar Road in Satara, Maharashtra. For over two decades, Jalsagar has been known for its delicious food, warm service and welcoming atmosphere that keeps locals and travelers coming back again and again. Situated conveniently near major roads and just a short distance from Satara city, Hotel Jalsagar offers a delightful dining experience whether you’re stopping by for lunch, dinner, or a casual treat with family and friends. At Jalsagar, you will find a wide variety of flavorsome dishes, including seafood specialties like crispy fish fry and surmai (kingfish) preparations, traditional Indian favorites, and flavorful dhaba classics. Many visitors praise the quality of food, generous portions, and friendly service — making it a well-liked choice among restaurants in the region. The ambience at Hotel Jalsagar combines a comfortable setting with scenic surroundings, including nearby views of lakes and greenery, adding to the overall relaxed dining experience. Whether you’re a local resident, a tourist visiting nearby attractions like Kanher Dam, or a passerby exploring Satara, Hotel Jalsagar promises great food, warm hospitality, and memorable meals every time you visit.
